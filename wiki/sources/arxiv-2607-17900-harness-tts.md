## Narrative

Lightweight **Harness Layer** wraps a TTS engine: closed-set stylistic prompt-tool registry + LLM planner (Qwen3-4B routing accuracies reported) → prompt-audio conditioned synthesis. Useful pattern for persona DM expressiveness without retraining TTS.
