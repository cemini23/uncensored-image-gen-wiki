## Narrative

Comprehensive **2024–2025 survey** covering **image, video, audio, and multimodal** deepfake **generation and detection** — first broad update spanning diffusion models and NeRF-era generators.

**Key findings for operators:**

| Topic | Survey claim |
|-------|----------------|
| Generation shift | GAN → **diffusion + identity adapters** (T2I/T2V, portrait animation, voice clone) |
| Detection gap | SOTA detectors **fail OOD** on fakes from unseen generators |
| **BioDeepAV** | New multimodal benchmark; HF dataset `University-of-Bucharest-BioDeep/BioDeepAV` |
| **POI detection** | Person-of-interest formulation — detect fakes of a *known* identity vs open-world |

### Workspace relevance

Inverse lens for persona ops: platforms and regulators deploy detectors that **do not generalize** to next-gen local stacks (Wan, Fish-Speech, FLUX LoRAs) — but **legal** and **platform-policy** risk remains high regardless (@concepts/persona-failure-modes.md).
