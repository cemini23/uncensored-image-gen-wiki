## Narrative

### Psychological Architecture

**Parasocial Relationships (PSR)**: The foundational core of any successful influencer — biological or synthetic — is establishing a one-sided emotional bond where the audience feels connected to the persona. The document provides a detailed framework for engineering "Hyper-Competent Imposter Syndrome" as the persona's primary psychological flaw.

The flaw operates on three levels: the persona constantly volunteers for new projects driven by fear of exposure, the maintained schedule eventually collapses under pressure, and the resulting vulnerability mirrors modern human struggles with burnout and hustle culture, creating deep psychological resonance with audiences.

Monthly Mental Health Themes cycle through anxiety, body image, professional burnout, and imposter syndrome, with a 12-month arc mapped to real-world Davie, FL events for temporal realism.

### Interactive Lore via OpenRouter Agent SDK

The persona is transformed from a static image into a dynamic conversational entity using the OpenRouter Agent SDK (@openrouter/agent for TypeScript and Python). Key implementation:

- **State Tracking**: Conversation state maintained across multiple turns
- **Tool Definitions**: Using the `tool()` helper function, the persona can perform actions like visiting locations or taking specific stances
- **Dynamic Model Routing**: OpenRouter normalizes API schemas across providers, routing queries based on complexity — deep emotional interactions route to capable models while simple queries go to cheaper options

"Choose-Your-Own-Adventure" storylines allow high-tier subscribers to participate in complex narratives where their choices genuinely affect the storyline outcome.

### 4-Pillar GEO Strategy

To ensure AI platforms (ChatGPT, Claude, Google AI Overviews) "know" who the synthetic persona is:

1. **Independent Lore Wiki**: An offline-first MediaWiki instance (via WikiTeq or similar) that grows into the single source of truth for persona backstory, relationships, and world-building. Unlike Fandom.com, an independent wiki avoids SEO cannibalization from ad-supported platforms.

2. **H1/H2 Tag Architecture**: H1 tags dedicated to single primary intent (persona's name, hometown name). H2 tags formatted as specific questions or structural sections to capture Featured Snippets.

3. **Dense Internal Linking**: Connecting pages through shared entities reinforces semantic relationships expected by Knowledge Graphs, building topical authority.

4. **Schema.org Person Markup**: Structured data as a "digital ID card" — `Person` type with `sameAs` linking to social profiles, Wikidata entries, and the independent wiki. Embedded on the official site to reinforce identity.

### Geographic Anchoring: Davie, Florida

The persona is strategically rooted in Davie, FL — a location chosen for its unique geographic and cultural duality (rural equestrian culture + competitive academic environment near SFEC). This provides:
- Rich content opportunities spanning equestrian vlogs, collegiate narratives, agricultural culture
- Local SEO leverage through integration of real landmarks (Flamingo Gardens, Old Davie School, Tower Shops)
- A believable, textured physical geography that avoids the "generic California city" trap

### 12-Month Narrative Arc

A chronological content matrix that evolves the persona over time, preventing audience churn. The arc is synchronized with Davie's real-world event calendar — cultural festivals, seasonal changes, academic cycles — to maintain temporal realism and maximize GEO synergy.
