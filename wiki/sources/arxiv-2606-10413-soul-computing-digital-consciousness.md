## Narrative

**Soul Computing** — theoretical framework for **digital agents with persistent self-identity**, distinct from chatbot RAG, affective computing, or historical-figure Q&A. Positions multimodal digital fragments (text logs, AV, behavioral metadata) as **reverse mappings** of consciousness construction for silicon-based reconstruction.

### Narrow vs broad

| Scope | Definition |
|-------|------------|
| **Narrow Soul Computing** | Core "pure-brain" agent: hierarchical memory + continuous self-identity + personality homeostasis — no carrier/UI |
| **Broad Soul Computing** | Narrow core + multimodal embodiment (avatar, voice, social presence) |

**Architecture (3 layers):** data-driven layer (fragment ingestion) → narrow computing core (self-model memory, endogenous motivation) → broad externalization (TTS, video avatar, platform presence).

### Distinction from wiki persona stack

Not a generative model paper — **ops/philosophy layer** above @concepts/persona-ops-stack.md. Clarifies why persona consistency (@concepts/persona-consistency-methods.md) + audio stack (@concepts/persona-audio-stack.md) matter beyond one-shot image gen: they are **externalization layers** around a claimed "intensional" core vs purely functional chat wrappers.

**Ethics:** paper flags five core challenges (data, algorithm, ethics) — relevant to monetized synthetic persona legal landscape but not technical ingest for ComfyUI.
