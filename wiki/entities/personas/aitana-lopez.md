## Raw Concept

Page prompted by Path A step 6 ingest of the persona-monetization survey docx (May 2026). Aitana Lopez is the most-documented Tier 1 AI persona — the archetype for agency-managed flagship monetization. Created late 2023 by Rubén Cruz and Diana Núñez Morales at The Clueless (Barcelona); scaled to >$20K/mo by May 2026. The Clueless subsequently launched a second persona (Maia), demonstrating roster scaling.

Synthesized from @sources/persona-monetization-2026.md.

## Narrative

### Origin and operator

- **Created**: late 2023
- **Operators**: Rubén Cruz + Diana Núñez Morales
- **Agency**: The Clueless (Barcelona)
- **Genesis motivation**: agency had experienced repeated project delays / cancellations attributed to unpredictability of human influencers. Aitana was designed to mitigate human logistical friction.

### Niches

- Fitness
- Gaming

Distribution: Instagram (top-of-funnel) + Fanvue (gated monetization).

### Earnings trajectory

| Date | Disclosed earnings | Notes |
|------|---------------------|-------|
| Late 2023 | €3,000/mo or €1,000/ad | Initial reports |
| May 2026 | $20,000+/mo | Brand partnerships + Fanvue subs/PPV/tips |

### Technical stack (per public reports)

- Generative image models optimized for photorealism (specific stack not publicly disclosed; community attribution to FLUX / SDXL fine-tunes era → moving to FLUX.2 era 2025-2026)
- Extensive **manual compositing via Photoshop** — not pure-AI pipeline, hybrid generation + retouch
- AI video generators: **Arcads** (text-to-presenter) cited
- Consistent content cadence without physical photoshoots / wardrobe changes

The hybrid generation + manual retouch approach foreshadowed the May 2026 modal stack: locked persona LoRA + identity adapter + multi-angle dataset → ComfyUI generation → human curation. → @concepts/persona-consistency-methods.md → @concepts/persona-content-cadence.md

### Roster scaling — Maia

The Clueless launched a second AI model named Maia, packaging multiple personas under a single operational umbrella to target different demographic segments. Demonstrates the scalability path from Tier 1 (single flagship) → multi-persona agency at the same operational layer.

### Why Aitana matters as a case study

1. **Earliest documented Tier 1 success**: predates much of the 2024-2025 boom; established the archetype.
2. **Agency-managed model**: shows the production-pipeline path (vs solo operator).
3. **Hybrid generation + retouch**: pre-figured the modal 2026 quality discipline (vs volume-flooding).
4. **Roster scaling demonstrated**: multi-persona under one ops umbrella is actually viable.
5. **Public-source documented**: Dataconomy, Economic Times, Influencer Marketing Hub citations make this one of the few persona case studies with multi-source verification.

### Build-track relevance

For the wiki's persona-track build orientation:
- Demonstrates the **revenue ceiling** at Tier 1 ($20K+/mo) given disciplined ops
- Validates **hybrid generation + manual retouch** vs pure-pipeline-automation as the quality path
- The fitness + gaming niche choice + cross-platform funnel (IG → Fanvue) is the modal Tier 1 distribution pattern

## Dead Ends

- **Pure-AI no-retouch pipeline at Tier 1 quality**: Aitana's stack uses extensive Photoshop compositing on top of generation. Pure-pipeline outputs typically don't reach Tier 1 brand-partnership quality without manual curation.
