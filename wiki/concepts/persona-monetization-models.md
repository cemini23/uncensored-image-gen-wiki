#### Tier 1 case study detail

**Aitana Lopez (The Clueless Agency, Barcelona).** Launched July 2023 by Rubén Cruz and Diana Núñez. Originally presented as a 25-year-old fitness model; ~392K Instagram followers by April 2026. Earnings: ~€3K/mo average, peaking at ~€10K in good months, per direct interview with Cruz (Euronews, Dec 2024; Forbes, Nov 2023; Business Insider, Nov 2023). Brand partners: Big (supplements), Razer, Amazon Spain, Freepik, Llongueras (Vogue Business, Apr 2026). Stack: Stable Diffusion / Midjourney-class diffusion + Photoshop compositing into real-world locations; tens-of-thousands-of-euros initial production cost. Second persona **Maia** demonstrates roster scaling.

**Emily Pellegrini.** Most widely covered NSFW-tier case. Launched late 2023; Fanvue November 2023. Anonymous operator. $9,688 first six weeks on Fanvue (Fanvue press release, Jan 2024, confirmed by Marca, Business Insider). $100K lifetime Fanvue earnings **confirmed on-record by Fanvue to 404 Media / Wired** (Nov 2024). Operator claims "seven figures in six months" via self-marketed "AI Influencer Accelerator" course — unverified by any third party. Fanvue told Wired Emily's on-platform usage had since become "extremely low" as the team pivoted to course sales.

**Lil Miquela (SFW context).** CGI/character-IP project by Brud studio (LA, 2016), not an AI-generated photo persona. Brud raised ~$6M seed from Sequoia (2018), reported $20-30M at $125M valuation. Estimated ~$8K/post sponsored rates, ~€8.96M annual revenue (The Cut / Bloomberg / OnBuy estimates, Nov 2020). Acquired by Dapper Labs 2021. Included as a comparative SFW data point only — economics are an order of magnitude removed from Fanvue creators.

### Revenue mechanics (where the money actually comes from)

Critical insight: **the bulk of mid-tier and Tier-1 revenue is NOT flat subscriptions** but per-interaction monetization:

- **DMs (parasocial messaging)**: NLP-augmented chat where the LLM parses and retains personal fan data (name, country, struggles, relationship status) to maintain intimacy at scale. Voice notes carry a high premium. Lexi Love (outlier) reportedly receives "20 marriage proposals per month".
- **PPV (pay-per-view) locked posts**: tipped per-unlock; high-margin and platform-encouraged.
- **Tips**: the natural extension of DM intimacy.
- **Subscriptions**: floor income; mostly an audience-qualification gate.
- **Brand partnerships** (Tier 1 only): Aitana initially €1,000/ad; Kenza Layli secured Hyundai + Bioderma sponsorships through 190K IG followers.

### ROI math

The reality of "passive income" claims vs disclosed cost structure:

```
Net = (Σ revenue streams) − (fixed costs + variable costs + gateway fees)
                        ÷
                     human oversight hours

Where:
  Fixed software/API:     $200-400/mo
  Traffic generation:     $300-1000/mo (ads, growth ops)
  Gateway fees:           15-20% of gross revenue
  High-risk fees:         $1,450/yr Visa/Mastercard registration
  Human oversight:        15-25 hours/week (mid-tier) up to 0.5-1.5 FTE/persona (Tier 1)
```

A mid-tier ($2-3K/mo gross) operator nets $800-1,500/mo after costs and 15-25 hours/week — **substantially below** marketing claims. Course costs ($27 to $247) plus secondary capital requirements ($500-1,400+/mo) put break-even at month 4-9 for a successful build, indefinitely for failed builds.

### Cost arbitrage via Model Routers

Sophisticated agencies dynamically route NLP tasks across models:
- Simple sentiment / categorization → GPT-4o-mini class ($0.15/M tokens range)
- Mid-complexity persona scripting → Claude 3.5 Sonnet / GPT-4o
- Complex multi-step persona arcs → Claude 4.6 / GPT-5.4

Reported infrastructure savings: 60-90%. This pattern is the operational equivalent of the FLUX-2 / Z-Image-Turbo tier arbitrage in the image generation track. → @concepts/persona-ops-stack.md

### Content cadence and revenue (the "AI slop" pivot)

Early-era operators flooded platforms with daily volume; 2025-2026 algorithmic suppression of AI slop forced a pivot to 3-5 high-quality posts/week with narrative progression, varied angles, realistic lighting. Quality cadence outperforms volume on retention and DM-conversion. → @concepts/persona-content-cadence.md

## Dead Ends

- **"Passive income" framing**: marketing-claim only. Real cost structure puts mid-tier break-even at month 4-9 with 15-25 hr/week.
- **Volume-flooding cadence**: AI-slop algorithms suppress this since 2025. Quality-cadence (3-5 posts/wk) outperforms.
- **Hosted-LLM persona scripting for NSFW**: blocked at platform level; route to local LLM.
